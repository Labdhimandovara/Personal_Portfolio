About Me

A third-year Electronics Engineering student focused on building responsive, user-friendly websites. Along with technology, I am passionate about art and use it as a way to express creative ideas.

I lead my department’s magazine team and have gained valuable experience through AIESEC, which helped me develop strong communication, teamwork, and strategic thinking skills. I am continuously learning, experimenting, and evolving—always eager to take on projects that challenge me to think differently.
